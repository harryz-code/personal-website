About

I cofounded Timelaps (timelaps.io), the first continuous, research-grade brand tracker, at 1/5th the price of traditional alternatives.

Previously, I cofounded HackQuest (hackquest.io), the leading AI + crypto developer education and hackathon platform with 2m+ users. When available, I continue my passion of championing other early stage builders by contributing to Protocol Labs' early builder programs and advising / investing in web3 and edtech startups.

Before committing to the founder path, I tried every role I could. Since age 15, I never stopped working. Life is like a simulation game, and I wanted to understand my talents and potentials as early as possible. I learned how to sell (coffee) by knocking on door after door. I got started in marketing by running paid search and social ads for TikTok after finishing four courses on Coursera. I experienced the growth of a startup from 20 to 300+ members, going from an intern to one of the heads. I also followed the wave and tried consulting, banking, software engineering, and data science: learned quickly, but always felt something was missing. After trying over 15 roles across sales, engineering, research and development, growth, strategy, and investing, I chose to build.

I love learning. Unlike many successful founders who dropped out, I finished school with academic honors and 172 academic credits (128 required for graduation), because my university charges the same tuition regardless. Considering learning a luxury, I became good at time-boxing.

I constantly think about work once I set my mind on the one thing, but I also enjoy playing table games (chess, poker, monopoly), cooking & eating (international cuisines), and ball sports (basketball, volleyball, tennis).

Changing the world starts with those around me. Work taught me to always "give first," and life taught me to always "be grateful." If I can be helpful in any way, please feel free to reach out.
